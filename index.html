Intro

I graduated from UNSW with a degree in Computational Design and have learnt a lot during my three year semester career, using software such as Rhino, Grasshopper, Python and BIM.Check out my work form work.

The first year was difficult for me, but I was very interested in modelling, parametric, I liked using Rhino and Grasshopper, and I was also very interested in architecture, and on my webpage I have summarised all my work over the last three years, using many different software.For each page I have selected what I feel are the best pieces to showcase each year.
